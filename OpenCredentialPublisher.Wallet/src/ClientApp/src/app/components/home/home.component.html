Welcome to the Teacher Portal

Here you can publish your ETS test results on a blockchain so that you may take your test results anywhere using a wallet app on your mobile device.

  1. Download the wallet app to your device
  2. Associate your wallet app with your account
  3. Enter your ETS Candidate Id(s)
  4. Verify your ETS data
  5. Select your ETS data that you want to publish
